You can change your view of the cube by clicking and dragging anywhere in the grey. To stop the view of the cube from spinning just click and release the left mouse button in the grey.


By clicking one of the three buttons below, you will set the degrees that the face will be rotated. (Default is 90)



By clicking one of the two buttons below, you will set the direction that the face will be rotated. (Default is clockwise)



By clicking one of the six buttons below, you will rotate the face of the cube where the color of the center piece on that face matches the color named on the button pressed.
